Etigei Exile⛨

Etigei Exile is the second version of the Etigeox mod for Mindustry. Starting development in June 2022 (448 days after the original Etigeox mod), it finally released in 2024, after four rewrites.

Terra Accura⛨

Terra Accura is a planned mod based around environmental destruction to harvest resources and upgrade a customizable core unit.

Rubiginosus Legacy⛨

Rubiginosus Legacy is a standalone mod archiving the old version of the Rubiginosus maps after their revamp in Etigei Exile.

Blastanium⛨

Blastanium a small mod adding Blastanium to Serpulo, Erekir, and Rubiginosus, if installed. Blastanium is a new powerful type of ammunition based on a meme created by the playtesting team of Etigei Exile.

Timber⛨

Timber (EDT) is a mod used as a dependency by the other ReversTeam mods for Mindustry.

It features environmental destruction of trees and pines in Serpulo and Erekir while adding timber. It is used for similar environmental gameplay features in Neoulandia and Rubiginosus.
