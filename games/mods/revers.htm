Revers Series⛨


Revers is a series of Minecraft modpacks developed by ReversTeam. Revers initially planned to feature six modpacks with their own lore, progression, gameplay, and quests, but only three were completed.

Revers - Base⛨

Revers - Base is the first modpack in the Revers Series. It is a unpublished modpack given to the members of the now defunct TUC for March 2022.

Revers: Reverse Collapse⛨

Revers: Reverse Collapse is the second modpack in the Revers Series. It is a 1.12.2 modpack centered around magic, technology, exploration and base-building, with integrated lore and progression.


Reverse Collapse takes place after the events of Revers - Base, focusing on the aftermath of the Foreign Deities' invasion. The player receives aid from the goddess Elisa in the form of quests and currency.


The modpack uses Reverscore to interconnect progression across multiple mods. It also adds miscelaneous helpful items and bosses integrated within the questline and lore.

Revers: Continuum Turbulence⛨

Revers: Continnum Turbulence is the second modpack in the Revers Series. It is a harder version of Reverse Collapse, with difficulty scaled up, the addition of various other mods and most prominently the break down of the world into small floating islands.


The modpack uses Reverscore to interconnect progression across multiple mods. The Continuum Turbulence verison of Reverscore uniquely adds ice-related tools and armor sets.

Concatenation Lite

Concatenation is a 1.20.1 kitchen sink modpack meant as an experience differing from Revers. The full Concatenation, planned with progression and an expanded tarot system, was never fully realized.

Unfinished Modpacks

Four planned modpacks have never been finished. They have either been cancelled or delayed indefinitely.


Revers: Operation Cube⛨

Operation Cube was a planned modpack based around Tikup's Serenity mod. It is a sequel to Revers: Reverse Collapse but occurs before Revers: Shattered Connexion.

After Elisa's betrayal at the end of Revers: Reverse Collapse, the Overworld's mobs were put under Elisa's influence, granting her control over most of the dimension following the demise of the rest of the Foreign Deities.

Revers: Deep Dive

Deep Dive was a planned modpack. The player delves into underworld dungeons to find and save Zeta to counter Elisa's control over the Overworld.

Revers: Shattered Connexion⛨

Revers: Shattered Connexion was a planned modpack, to be integrated with Team Citrus' Starset mod. Elisa was planned to be featured as a bossfight for the finale fo the Revers series.

Concatenation

Concatenation was a planned modpack for 1.20.1 to feature a unique tarot choice system to define the entire game.
