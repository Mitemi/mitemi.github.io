Revers Modpack

"Heaven burns red"

Revers is a series of 1.12.2 Minecraft modpacks based around some kind of story and questline.
Revers: Base is the original Revers modpack that is not available to the public. It was originally created for March break for TUC and doesn't feature any story or quests, a pack of mods.
Revers: Reverse Collapse is the main modpack of the Revers series and features relatively balanced difficulty, a myriad of quests and a small story which leaves a lot to the imagination.
Revers: Continuum Turbulence is the harder version of Reverse Collapse featuring harder increased difficulty, slightly more quests and mods.
None of these modpacks are currently supported anymore.
