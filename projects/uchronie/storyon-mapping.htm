Storyon's Mapping

Storyon's Mapping is an animated mapping series in Countryballs published on YouTube.

This series details the events of each of Storyon's Tales within the Parallel Pillars.

Mitemi's Alternate History of Europe

Alternate History of Europe is the first tale of Storyon's Mapping, set in an alternate 13th century Europe with the point of divergence being the middle of the 9th century.

Manche

Manche is set in a late-Victorian Era world, dominated by the Dual Monarchy and the Russian Empire. The Great War had just ended, resulting in a defeat of Spain and the Holy Roman Empire, both falling to dictatorship.

Polska Dominacja

Polska Dominacja is set in a Polish-dominated Europe on the aftermath of a Great War resulting in the fall of France.

Fallen Afterstorm

Fallen Afterstorm (Après la tempête tombée) is set in the aftermath of an alternate First World War which resulted in the collapse of all of its belligerents.

Galactic Council

Galactic Council is the final tale of Storyon's Mapping
