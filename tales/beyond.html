Flip: Beyond

Beyond the end and rebirth.

Flip: Beyond is a canonical story taking place after the events of Flip. After Yuma's defeat, Athifaenos flees from the victory party and hides as a student in a noble academy in the north of Fantai. A goddess of life and death as a student is sure to stir things up, especially when Yuma's Remnant begins to infect one of its honour students.
